Town of San Salvador De Jujuy

Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to promote a financial activity, encouraging people to show their small or medium businesses on this website, as well as their ventures. Also this website will show the main cities of San Salvador de Jujuy so that tourism increases in the city. Thus, this website will provide and encourage people to join the chamber of commerce of the city of Jujuy, showing activities and events that will be held in the city so that more is known about what is done in this chamber of commerce.

ssjujuy.com

Visit the website ssjujuy.com where you can find more information about the great community we have. You can learn more about it, and get in touch with the members of the Jujuy Chamber of Commerce.
