Target Audience

The target audience of the Jujuy Chamber of Commerce will be mainly entrepreneurs, those who have their medium or small business and want to make it known, and help each other. Another audience will be the general public of the city of Jujuy and other cities, encouraging tourism in the city, holding events and activities to meet entrepreneurs, and more about the Jujuy Chamber of Commerce.

Scenarios

People will visit the website looking for events and activities that take place on a weekly basis. They will seek to interact and look at other people's endeavors. Tourism and trade relations will be encouraged, through detailed information on the undertakings, tourist places, and images.

  • What types of events can I find about the chamber of commerce?
  • How can I improve my business?
  • What kind of enterprises are there around Jujuy?
  • Who are part of the Jujuy Chamber of Commerce?
  • What weather is there in the state of Jujuy weekly?

Personas

Entrepreneurs: One of the target audiences will be small and medium-sized entrepreneurs, so that in this way they can be members of the Jujuy Chamber of Commerce, advertising their enterprises, and generating business relationships between them. Entrepreneurs will normally be over 18 years old, being able to access this website through mobile devices, tablets, or laptops. The entrepreneurial audience will have a medium-high income, and thus they will be encouraged to generate business relationships between entrepreneurs.

Tourists: Another of the audiences will be the people who live in Jujuy and those who live outside of Jujuy, to generate more tourism in the city, through events and activities, making the chamber of commerce known. Tourists who will access the chamber of commerce site will normally be over 16 years old, and will be able to access this site from any device. The tourist audience could be entire families, or young people who want to visit Jujuy.
