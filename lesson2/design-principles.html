Proximity

amazon store

amazon.com

On the main page of Amazon, we can see the different topics that Amazon offers, as well as on this page they offer products for sale. Each of these topics, apps, or products for sale, is accompanied by a textual description, review, image, price in some cases. Each of the features appears to the right of the main image, in close proximity to the image it describes. You can easily differentiate how each group is slightly separated from the other groups, but at the same time they form a whole, which are separated even more differently from the header, comments, or footer. In this way each related items should be grouped close together. This provides the viewer with a visual cue that these items are similar and should be treated as a group.

Contrast

PlayStation Store

store.playstation.com

On the main page of PlayStation Store, a little below the offers, you can see some topics such as the new console, games, discounts, etc. These are general items that can be accessed to see more in detail what it contains. In this way you can see a great difference with the rest of the page, because there is a great contrast between the white and blue color of the main page, and the black color for the general topics. Therefore, what is represented here is that thanks to contrast it is easy to make dis-similar elements very different. Contrast is one of the most powerful visual design tools. This can be seen throughout the PlayStation page, because they also use the color blue to focus people's eyes on the subscribe button. Also, many times they make offers to the public, and these offers are normally yellow, a totally different color from the one used by PlayStation but that helps to differentiate it from other items.

Alignment

Booking

Booking.com

On the main page of booking.com you can see that the alignment of the items, hotels, user registration page as seen in the image, is aligned correctly, and there is a connection between them. The topic of hotels, lodgings, offers are correctly aligned. When you enter to see a particular hotel, the characteristics of the hotels, such as the description, score, reviews, comments, are correctly aligned, the texts can be read clearly and smoothly, and it is not appreciated that any element is poorly designed, or misaligned with respect to the other elements. This way every visual element on this page has some kind of connection to at least one other element. Nothing is positioned haphazardly.
