Membership
Membership Levels
Description

Bronze Membership

Bronze membership costs only 10% more than normal, including many benefits. The number of ads you can do weekly will be 1 times. Trainings, meetings and training will have a 10% discount. The events you attend will have a 10% discount.

Gold Membership

Gold membership costs only 50% more than normal, including many benefits. The number of ads you can do weekly will be 3 times. Trainings, meetings and trainings will have a 50% discount, and you will be given one free training per month. The events you attend will have a 50% discount.

NP Membership

If you own a non-profit organization, you can access all the benefits for free. You will have access to the events that take place monthly for free. Your organization can be trained twice a month for free. And you are offered the opportunity to publish your organization once a week for free.

Silver Membership

Silver membership costs only 30% more than normal, including many benefits. The number of ads you can do weekly will be 2 times. Trainings, meetings and training will have a 30% discount. The events you attend will have a 30% discount.
